# Esmifer Bicicletas

## Company Overview

**Esmifer Bicicletas** is a family-owned company from **Ocotlán, Jalisco, Mexico**, a region known for its woodworking excellence and furniture manufacturing tradition.  
The company combines artisan quality with product innovation by developing one of the **first and only wooden bicycles in Latin America**.

This project is positioned as a premium mobility and destination-enhancement solution for public and private partners seeking distinctive, sustainable, and high-value experiences.

---

## History & Heritage

Esmifer was born from generations of craftsmanship dedicated to transforming wood into durable, elegant, and functional products.  
Built on Ocotlán’s legacy in furniture production, the company applies proven woodworking techniques, precision manufacturing, and design culture to a new mobility category.

Its identity reflects:

- Family-driven leadership
- High-quality artisanal execution
- Local Mexican manufacturing pride
- Long-term vision for regional and international partnerships

---

## Wooden Bicycle Innovation

Esmifer designed and produced a unique wooden bicycle platform that merges:

- Structural engineering and handcrafted woodwork
- Premium aesthetics for high-visibility environments
- Comfortable urban and recreational riding experience
- Differentiation for tourism and place branding

This innovation creates a signature product with strong storytelling value for destinations, hospitality groups, and public programs.
